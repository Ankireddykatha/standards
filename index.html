Disaster Recovery Standards

Tier 1 — Mission Critical
RTO< 15 min
RPO< 1 min
  • Multi-region active-active deployment
  • Real-time database replication (synchronous)
  • Automated failover with health checks
  • Global load balancing with DNS failover
Tier 2 — Business Critical
RTO< 1 hour
RPO< 15 min
  • Active-passive with warm standby
  • Asynchronous database replication
  • Automated backup every 15 minutes
  • Infrastructure as Code for rapid rebuild
Tier 3 — Standard
RTO< 4 hours
RPO< 1 hour
  • Pilot light / cold standby setup
  • Scheduled snapshots and backups
  • Terraform for environment re-provisioning
  • Documented runbooks and DR playbooks
